Saslaw, M., Yang, D., Lee, D., Poulsen, C. J., & Henkes, G. A. 2024. An Isotope Mass Balance Analysis of Evaporative Loss From Lake Turkana, Kenya Using δ18O and δD of Natural Waters. Water Resources Research, 60(6). https://doi.org/10.1029/2023WR036076

We present new water isotope data from the Turkana Basin along with a novel Bayesian approach for modeling lake water evaporation using isotopes.
